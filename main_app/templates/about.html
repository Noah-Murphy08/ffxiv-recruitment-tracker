About

The FFXIV Recruitment Tracker is an app designed for people that want to take part in group content but don't know where to look.

  • Community Focused: Become part of a friendly, active group of adventurers dedicated to exploring Eorzea together.
  • All Playstyles Welcome: Whether you're a seasoned raider, casual player, or crafting enthusiast, there's a place for you here.
  • Easy Access: Create a libary of your own players and browse through our community of players to see who you can recruit!
